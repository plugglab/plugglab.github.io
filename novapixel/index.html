NovaPixel

A modern, low-distraction PvP texture pack — sharper hit indicators, clean UI, and consistent lighting for competitive play.

Download Pack Discord Support

Capabilities

What NovaPixel does

NovaPixel strips away visual noise so every fight comes down to reflexes, not rendering.

Clear Hit Indicators

High-contrast damage and hit animations make combat readable even in fast 1v1s and team fights.

Performance First

Simplified textures and reduced animation frames keep frame rates high on low-end hardware.

Consistent Lighting

Flattened block lighting removes harsh shadows that can hide players in doorways and caves.

Clean UI Overlay

Redesigned hotbar, health, and hunger icons stay legible during chaotic fights.

Fast-Swing Animations

Weapon swing timing is tuned to feel snappier without breaking vanilla combat mechanics.

Regularly Updated

Texture tweaks continue based on Discord feedback from active PvP testers.
