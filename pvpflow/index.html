What PvPFlow does

PvPFlow is built for servers running classic 1.8 combat mechanics and the players who grew up on them.

Classic Swing Feel

Sword and axe animations are tuned to match the snappy rhythm of 1.8-era PvP.

No-Cooldown Visuals

Attack indicator bar is hidden by default so it never gets in the way on cooldown-disabled servers.

Sharpened Weapon Models

Redrawn sword and bow textures stay readable at a glance during fast combos.

Reduced Particle Clutter

Combat particles are toned down so hits and misses stay easy to track in group fights.

Minimal HUD

Health, hunger, and armor icons shrink slightly to keep more of the screen focused on the fight.

Tournament Ready

Used as the default pack in community PvPFlow test tournaments — balanced for fairness, not flash.
