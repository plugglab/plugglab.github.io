SafeTrade

A clean, two-sided trade window with mandatory confirmation and inventory validation — no more scammed trades.

Download on Modrinth Discord Support

Capabilities

What SafeTrade does

SafeTrade replaces risky drop-and-grab trading with a locked, mutual-confirmation trade window.

Locked Trade Window

Both players place items into a shared window that locks the moment either side confirms — no last-second item swaps.

Double Confirmation

A trade only completes once both players explicitly confirm the final offer, with a visible countdown to review it.

Inventory Validation

SafeTrade checks inventory space and item stack limits before finalizing, so no items vanish into a full inventory.

Distance-Safe Trading

Trade requests work across short render distances without requiring players to stand directly next to each other.

Trade Logging

Every completed trade is logged for admins, making scam disputes easy to resolve with a clear paper trail.

Simple Setup

One command to send a trade request, one GUI to complete it — no economy plugin required.
